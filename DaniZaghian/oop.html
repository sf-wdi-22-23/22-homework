Q: What is the difference between a constructor and a prototype? A: A constructor creates a new instance of an object with certain undefined properties and methods, while a prototype is an inheritance instance where the desendants stem from a prototype object. Q: What does this mean inside a constructor? A: 'This' refers to the new object being created.
